Individualized Strength-Based Programming

  • ✔ We use an asset based lens to create an individualized monthly or yearly plan for each client to live their lives to their fullest potential.
  • ✔ We collaborate with our client’s current team to provide unique and individual supports and services to assist our client’s in meeting their life goals.
  • ✔ We meet with our clients, their families, and their care teams to develop goals.
  • ✔ We provide an individualized visual, digital, and/or written care plan that outlines the goals and action plan.
  • ✔ We meet all clients and their caregivers monthly or at the discretion of the client and caregivers to review goals, celebrate successes, and create next steps.

Personalized Outreach Services

  • ✔ We help individuals to increase independence and participation in their community
  • ✔We organize and transport individuals to medical and mental health appointments.
  • ✔We help connect individuals to income sources, health care, housing, and community services.
  • ✔ We provide meaningful community outings and opportunities to individuals
  • ✔ We provide life skills to those who require assistance with hygiene, meal preparation, budgeting, shopping, dressing etc
  • ✔We provide social group community outings that provide opportunities for positive and healthy peer connections and development of social skills
  • ✔ We assist individuals with navigating through their emotions and practicing self-regulation and coping skills in situations that are uniquely applicable to them.

Living Our Best Life Adult Day Program

  • Our Living Our Best Life Adult Day Program offers a therapeutic program for adults with disabilities and/or mental health needs within the Hamilton community. We offer a structured, planned day out in a supportive and safe environment. This day program focuses on social, recreational, and engaging positive activities to assist those that struggle with emotional control and self-regulation.
  • Our highly qualified staff provides engaging and meaningful community activities to promote physical, mental, and social healthy living where we strive towards helping our participants live their best lives.

Crisis Interventions

  • ✔ We are open 24 hours to provide support to individuals in crisis
  • ✔We will create safety plans in collaboration with community partners and families
  • ✔Our staff is ASIST Suicide Prevention Trained
